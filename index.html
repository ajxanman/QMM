Welcome to the center of the questumation mark movement!

What is a questumation mark?

A questumation mark is a form of punctuation for separating sarcastic sentences from serious ones! You simply add it at the end of a sentence just like a period, and people will know you're being sarcastic! For example: "I don't need to pack clothes for my five day road trip(questumation mark)".

What is the goal of the questumation mark movement?

Our goal is to make the questumation mark available on all keyboards! We feel that this would be a very usefull mark of punctuation that people would use on an everyday basis!

Why should it be incorporated in keyboards around the world?

It would be very usefull in many situations. When speaking, the tone of your voice shows how you mean what you're saying, but that can be very difficult when typing! With a questumation mark, you can more easily be sarcastic in texts, emails, and any other time you're using a keyboard!
